This event prompted him to pursue a career in music as a means of escape and self-expression.

In 2014, 21 Savage released his debut single, “Picky,” which garnered attention and laid the foundation for his future success. However, it was his collaboration with producer Metro Boomin on the mixtape “Savage Mode” in 2016 that propelled him into the spotlight. The mixtape received critical acclaim and led to a record deal with Epic Records.

With the support of a major label, 21 Savage released his debut studio album “Issa Album” in 2017. The album’s lead single, “Bank Account,” became a breakout hit and solidified his position as a rising star in the rap industry. His subsequent albums, including “I Am > I Was,” showcased his growth as an artist and cemented his place among the top talents in the genre.
