Corey Haim took the world by storm as a child actor who quickly rose to fame after only a handful of television and movie roles. Haim made his breakthrough in Lucas (1986), which would be revealed years later as the actor's most traumatic role. Not all famous children survive the strains of Hollywood, and Corey Haim was certainly not the first or last to be scarred by a childhood in the spotlight.

Despite Haim's overwhelming success as a critically-acclaimed, award-winning child actor, Haim did not make it out of child stardom unscathed. But Haim was always supported by his close friend and fellow actor Corey Feldman, who has bravely spoken out about the abuse both of them suffered as children in Hollywood.

Corey Haim's Heartbreaking Story

According to Feldman, there is a huge problem in Hollywood that involves child stars being taken advantage of. In 2020, Corey Feldman's documentary debuted at a screening in Los Angeles, and in it, Feldman named names of the men who allegedly sexually assaulted him and Corey Haim. According to Corey Feldman, Charlie Sheen assaulted Haim during the making of the 1986 film Lucas.

It took Corey Feldman a lot to get his documentary to be viewed by the public, after no streaming services were interested in airing the documentary. The "Goonies" actor showed the documentary on his website, and as a result, Corey Feldman was in fear for his life.

Corey Feldman has provided a voice for his friend Corey Haim, who sadly passed away in 2010. The two Coreys were best friends for a very long time, their first movie together being The Lost Boys.

The pair went on to be in twelve movies together and even had their own show together called The Two Coreys.

In his final moments, Haim was with his mother, and told her that he didn't feel well. The Lost Boys actor was initially believed to have died of a drug overdose after it was well known that he had struggled with addiction for most of his adult life, but the truth is Corey Haim died of pneumonia.

His death shook fans and devastated everyone who knew him, and was especially heartbreaking after rumors started to circulate about what the last years of his life were like, and about the abuse he suffered as a child.

What Was The Final Year Of Haim's Life Like?

Corey Haim faded from fame. He was addicted to prescription drugs and despite visiting rehab multiple times, Corey kept relapsing.

Apparently, Haim was alone in adulthood and didn't have a lot of money, and even drifted away from Corey Feldman, who decided that [Feldman] and Haim should take a break from their friendship for a year while Haim focused on his sobriety and recovery.

Corey Haim will forever be missed, his story a reminder of the dark side of Hollywood and why Feldman's mission to be the voice of those affected by the dark side of Hollywood is so important.
