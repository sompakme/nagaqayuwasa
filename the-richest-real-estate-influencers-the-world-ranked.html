A real estate influencer is an individual who has gained significant recognition, credibility, and a following within the real estate industry due to their expertise, success, and ability to engage with their audience. These influencers often share their knowledge, insights, and experiences through various platforms such as social media, blogs, podcasts, and speaking engagements. The world of real estate has produced some of the wealthiest and most influential people in history. These individuals have amassed incredible fortunes and inspired countless others to follow in their footsteps.

From humble beginnings to global empires, real estate influencers have left an indelible mark on the industry and continue to shape its future as well as theirs by amassing stunning net worths through their hard work.

Raphael Vargas - $5 Million

Raphael Vargas is a self-made real estate entrepreneur who started his journey at 21. In his early days, he went through numerous social and life-threatening turmoil, which included getting thrown out of high school and a murder attempt on him, as per Insider. Today, he is a sought-after speaker and mentor, sharing his knowledge and experience with aspiring real estate investors. Vargas has almost 500K followers on Instagram and has already built himself a $5 Million net worth.

Chad Carroll - $10 Million

Chad Carroll is a luxury real estate agent and star of the hit reality TV show, Million Dollar Listing Miami. With over a decade of experience in the industry, Carroll has built a reputation for his exceptional negotiation skills and unparalleled market knowledge. His impressive client list includes celebrities, athletes, and high-net-worth individuals. With a net worth of $10 million, Chad Carroll has solidified his place among the top real estate influencers in the world.

Cody Sperber - $18 Million

Cody Sperber, also known as the Clever Investor as per Entrepreneur, is a real estate investor, entrepreneur, and educator. He began his career in real estate with no money or experience and quickly became one of the most successful investors in the United States. Sperber has since dedicated his life to teaching others how to achieve financial freedom through real estate investing. With a net worth of $18 million and a whopping 1.1 Million followers on Instagram, Cody Sperber is a shining example of the potential for success in the real estate industry.

Fredrik Eklund - $30 Million

Fredrik Eklund is a Swedish-born real estate broker, author, and television personality. Best known for his role on Bravo's Million Dollar Listing New York, Eklund has become one of the most recognizable faces in the industry. With a net worth of $30 million, he has built a successful career representing high-profile clients and selling some of the most luxurious properties in New York City. Eklund's charisma and expertise have made him a sought-after expert and influencer in the world of real estate, with a massive 1.4 Million followers on Instagram.

Ryan Serhant - $40 Million

Ryan Serhant is a top-producing real estate broker, author, and Million Dollar Listing New York star. He cracked an $8.5 Million deal in his second year as a realtor and has already amassed a net worth of $40 million, as per Wealthy Gorilla. Serhant has built an impressive real estate empire that includes his brokerage, SERHANT. Known for his innovative marketing strategies and relentless work ethic, Serhant, with his massive 1.5 Million followers on Instagram, has become a leading figure in the industry and an inspiration to aspiring real estate professionals worldwide.

Barbara Corcoran - $100 Million

Barbara Corcoran is a real estate mogul, author, and television personality best known for being an investor on the hit show Shark Tank. With a massive net worth of $100 million, Corcoran has built a successful career as the founder of The Corcoran Group, one of New York City's premier real estate firms. She sold The Corcoran Group to NRT for $66 million in 2001as per Amra & Elma. Her keen business sense and ability to spot potential in entrepreneurs have made her a respected figure in both the real estate and investment worlds.

Mauricio Umansky - $100 Million

CEO and founder of one of the country's most prestigious real estate brokerages: The Agency, Mauricio Umansky, is one of the most well-known faces in real estate. With a net worth of $100 million, Umansky has built a reputation for exceptional service and expertise in the high-end real estate market. His impressive client list includes celebrities, royalty, and business magnates. Umansky's success and influence in the industry have made him a sought-after expert and speaker on luxury real estate.

Grant Cardone - $600 Million

Grant Cardone, the wealthiest and most followed influencer on this list, is a real estate investor, entrepreneur, and best-selling author with a net worth of $600 million. As the founder of Cardone Capital, he has built a real estate portfolio worth over $2 billion. Cardone is also a renowned motivational speaker and sales trainer, inspiring millions worldwide to succeed personally and professionally. His vast wealth and whopping 3.2 Million followers on Instagram have made him one of the most prominent figures in real estate.

The world's eight wealthiest real estate influencers have ever seen have achieved incredible success through hard work, determination, and a passion for their industry. Their stories inspire those looking to make their mark in the world of real estate. As the industry evolves, these influencers will continue to shape its future and inspire the next generation of real estate professionals. Grant Cardone stands out as the wealthiest influencer on the list, with an impeccable net worth of $600 Million.
