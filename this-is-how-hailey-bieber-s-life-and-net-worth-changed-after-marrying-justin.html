This Is How Hailey Bieber's Life And Net Worth Changed After Marrying Justin

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Hailey Bieber seems to be everywhere you look these days. Her image is sprawled across billboard ads, in print on magazine covers, and of course, she invades social media channels regularly with modeling posts that depict her in haute couture and images of her endless love for her husband, Justin Bieber. With a combined net worth of $310 million, it's hard not to notice this power couple.

Hailey's life has always been a privileged one, but Elle Magazine has reported on how dramatically Hailey's life has changed since becoming a Bieber, and it's hard to deny that this has been a serious upgrade, in the financial sense. However, life with Justin has also exposed Hailey to a series of issues that haven't been quite as easy to contend with. Here's how Hailey Bieber's life and finances have changed since marrying her internationally known pop-star hubby, Justin Bieber.

10 She Became A Homebody

Interestingly enough, while most people would assume that marrying such a wealthy celebrity would mean a lifestyle of jet-setting and endless parties, the opposite has been true for Hailey Bieber. Reports indicate that since marrying Justin, she has really noticed a change within herself. She greatly enjoys being a homebody and prefers to stay home and watch Friends reruns with Justin any chance she gets.

9 Hailey Bieber Had To Care For Justin Amid Health Issues

That whole "in sickness and in health" part of Hailey's marriage vows was tested right from the beginning of her marriage to Justin. Sadly, his health wasn't great during their first year of marriage, and this put a huge stress on the very young newlyweds. At the time, Justin was very ill, but he hadn't received a diagnosis. Now, they're aware that he suffers from Lyme disease, and Hailey was by his side every step of the way.

8 She Fell Victim To 'Jelena Fans'

Things clearly didn't work out between Justin Bieber and Selena Gomez, but some fans just haven't let go of the fact that they were just not destined to be together. There are some 'Jelena' fans out there that remain very angered by the fact that Justin married Hailey, and as a result, Hailey has had to contend with a lot of haters who interfere with her life and try to make her public appearances with Justin an absolute nightmare.

7 Her Public Exposure Increased Dramatically

Hailey Bieber had a lot of public exposure prior to marrying Justin Bieber, as both a ballet dancer, a model, and the sheer lineage of being born into the Baldwin family. However, when she tied the knot with Justin, her public exposure increased dramatically. She saw a massive spike in online followers and has since continued to take the spotlight to a far greater degree than when she was a single lady.

6 Hailey Bieber Has Seen A Huge Increase In Her Net Worth

Hailey Bieber has been the mastermind behind her own success and has truly made a mark for herself. She is actively involved in a variety of different projects and has earned herself a cool $20 million net worth on her own. However, Justin's net worth is valued at roughly $290 million, so their combined net worth as a married couple has truly afforded her an entirely different lifestyle than the one she was previously capable of leading.

5 Endorsements Started Rolling In

Being the wife of Justin Bieber has correlated to everyone wanting a piece of Hailey. Brand endorsements started rolling in, and Hailey's earning power changed drastically. Yahoo reports that "in 2021 alone, Bieber has appeared the covers of Vogue, V, Bazaar and GQ magazines. She did a major spread interview with Elle in January, was featured in the Superga spring-summer 2021 campaign and walked in the fall 2021 Moschino show."

4 She Has A Whole Lot More Real Estate

Justin's wildly enormous fortune has enabled Hailey to live a very different lifestyle than the one she was accustomed to. While her lifestyle has always been a high-end one, and she always had a huge chunk of money at her disposal, marrying someone as wealthy as Justin Bieber has led to Hailey's ability to live loudly. The lovebirds have gobbled up a whole lot of real estate, including a $25.8 million mansion in Beverly Hills that boasts features such as an infinity swimming pool, a koi pond and an expansive footprint of 2.5 acres.

3 She's Living A Very 'Adult Life'

Hailey Bieber is awfully young to be anyone's wife, and this has forced this 24-year-old to live a very adult lifestyle. She has grown up very fast and has been experiencing a full-blown marriage at an age when most of her friends are partying, hooking up, and still trying to discover their goals in life. She has declared that she's happy to be so grounded, but fans can't help but notice her lifestyle involves a whole lot more 'adulting' than usual for someone her age.

2 She Has Toughened Up

Being Justin Bieber's wife has taught Hailey how to toughen up. The media hasn't always been kind to her, and now that she is forced to contend with Justin Bieber's wild fan base every time she steps out of her house, she had been forced to grow a thick skin. Justin's fans are relentlessly vocal, and she has had to contend with a lot of media scrutiny.

1 Hailey Bieber Has Relied Heavily On Her Religion

One thing that Hailey and Justin have in common is their heavy reliance on their faith. They both center themselves around their church and their faith, and it is this religious connection that has truly bonded them together. Hailey takes her vows seriously and has truly been by Justin's side "in sickness and in health." When things get tough between Justin and Hailey, they seem to fall back on their faith, which keeps them together.
