Highlights

Leonardo DiCaprio has not only developed a reputation for exclusively dating women in their twenties but also for being an eternal bachelor, as many believe the alleged womanizer doesn’t desire to settle down. However, engagement rumors continue circulating around Leo and his new girlfriend, Vittoria Ceretti, suggesting he will tie the knot sooner rather than later, especially since it appears he introduced Vittoria to his father recently.

Why Fans Think Leonardo Could Be Engaged To His Much-Younger Girlfriend

Photographed leaving the popular Beverly Hills restaurant Funke after a late-night dinner, Leonardo, 49, and Vittoria were spotted alongside Leo's father, writer George DiCaprio, 80, and his stepmother, Peggy Ann Farrar.

Despite the fancy establishment, Leo donned a casual ensemble for the evening out, including a black bomber jacket paired with a matching t-shirt and baseball cap. He completed his look with jeans, sneakers, a gold chain, and even a face mask.

For her part, Vittoria, 25, an Italian model, went for a casual but cool look, wearing a black leather jacket layered over a form-fitting top, complemented by faded jeans and slip-ons.

Even Leonardo’s dad, George, looked stylish with his styled over his shoulders, adorned in a suede blazer and spectacles. Likewise, the actor’s step-mother wore a white turban and shawl for a sophisticated look.

While this could’ve marked the first time Leonardo introduced his much-younger girlfriend to his father, Vittoria wasn’t seen wearing the ring that sparked engagement rumors earlier this month. The model was seen wearing what appears to be a diamond on her ring finger while having dinner with Leo in Santa Monica at the start of April.
