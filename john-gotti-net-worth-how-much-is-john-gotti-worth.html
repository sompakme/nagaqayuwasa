John Gotti’s Early Life

John Gotti, the infamous mob boss, was born on October 27, 1940, in the Bronx, New York City. He experienced a challenging upbringing, growing up in poverty as one of ten siblings. Gotti’s parents, of Italian descent, struggled to make ends meet, with his father working as a day laborer.

From an early age, Gotti was drawn into a life of organized crime. He dropped out of school and joined the Fulton-Rockaway Boys, a gang associated with the mafia. This marked the beginning of his association with the criminal underworld.

Gotti’s rise through the ranks of organized crime was swift.
