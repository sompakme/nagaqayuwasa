The future roars loud for Disney's live-action adaptations, and expectations for Mufasa: The Lion King are sky-high.

This photo-realistic animated film serves as both a prequel and sequel, exploring the past that shaped Mufasa, king of the Pride Lands, while also hinting at the future that awaits Simba, his son.

The timeless appeal of the original animated film almost guarantees success, while the 2019 remake's box office gross of over $1.6 billion sets a high bar for the upcoming Mufasa: The Lion King.

Mufasa: The Lion King's cast brings together a stellar lineup of talents who have proven their mettle in various facets of the entertainment industry:

Veterans like Donald Glover, Seth Rogen, Billy Eichner, and John Kani, along with Beyoncé Knowles-Carter, reprise their roles from the 2019 remake, promising continuity and depth.

They are joined by newcomers such as Mads Mikkelsen, Thandiwe Newton (previously known as "Thandie Newton,") and Blue Ivy Carter in her feature film debut.

As anticipation builds, the net worth and careers of these distinguished actors underscore the film's high-caliber production and the financial prowess it represents.

Let's take a look at the net worths of Mufasa: The Lion King's cast of talented voice actors.

7 Keith David (Masego)

Keith David's Net Worth (Estimated) - $8 Million

The casting of Keith David, a legendary voice actor, as Mufasa's father in Disney's most anticipated live-action film of the year is a stroke of sonic brilliance.

He made his film debut in the 1982 classic The Thing, and in three decades, he has appeared in a remarkable tally of more than 300 roles across film, television, stage, and interactive media.

His films, including hits like Armageddon, Barbershop, The Chronicles Of Riddick, and Cloud Atlas, have collectively amassed over $4.5 billion at the global box office.

Thanks to his diverse career and consistent work, David has amassed an estimated net worth of $8 million.

6 Billy Eichner (Timon)

Billy Eichner's Net Worth (Estimated) - $8 Million

Returning as the humorous meerkat Timon in Mufasa: The Lion King, Billy Eichner continues to bring his distinctive comedic style to one of Disney's most cherished franchises.

Eichner first made waves with his energetic and innovative game show Billy On The Street, which he created, starred in, and produced.

Eichner's career in Hollywood has expanded remarkably since his days as a stage show host in New York.

He boasts over 25 acting credits, including notable appearances in Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising, Most Likely To Murder, and several seasons of the highly-rated camp-fest American Horror Story.

In 2022, Eichner took a revolutionary step in film by writing, executive producing, and starring in Bros, the first gay romantic comedy by a major studio to feature an all-LGBTQ principal cast.

Bros grossed $14.8 million worldwide against a production budget of $22 million. Eichner's voice acting includes performances in Penguins Of Madagascar and The Angry Birds Movie, contributing to a career box office gross of over $2.5 billion.

Billy Eichner has an estimated net worth of $8 million.

5 Thandiwe Newton (Eshe)

Thandiwe Newton's Net Worth (Estimated) - $14 Million

Thandiwe Newton's film career took off in 1991 with Flirting, setting the stage for a series of impactful performances.

Her breakthrough role came in 1994 in Interview With The Vampire, which was soon followed by notable appearances in major franchises like Mission: Impossible and the Star Wars series.

Newton delivered an Emmy-winning performance as Maeve Millay in the HBO series Westworld, where she portrays a sentient android.

Thandie Newton earned $100,000 per episode for the first season of Westworld, before her salary increased to $250,000 per episode.

Over her three-decade career, Newton has appeared in over 30 films, taking on leading roles in 18 of them, with her collective projects grossing more than $2.5 billion at the global box office.

Outside her screen roles, Thandiwe is recognized for her impeccable style and influence, having been named one of the most influential women in the United Kingdom.

Thandiwe Newton's numerous accolades and box-office successes have propelled her to an estimated net worth of $14 million.

4 Mads Mikkelsen (Kiros)

Mads Mikkelsen's Net Worth (Estimated) - $14 Million

Mads Mikkelsen's ascent to the global spotlight began in his native Denmark, with a standout role in the Pusher film trilogy.

Since then, he has starred in over 27 films, which have collectively amassed $4.1 billion at the box office.

Mikkelsen's international breakthrough came in 2006 with the role of Le Chiffre, the villainous mastermind in Casino Royale, the twenty-first James Bond film.

He further introduced himself to global audiences with his portrayal of Dr. Hannibal Lecter in the widely acclaimed television series Hannibal, appearing in 39 episodes.

In 2020, Warner Bros. replaced Johnny Depp with Mikkelsen for the role of Grindelwald in Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets Of Dumbledore, and the film grossed over $407 million worldwide.

In Mufasa: The Lion King, the Danish actor will be bringing a whole new character to life, taking on the role of Kiros.

His ventures into the fashion industry also complement his earnings from acting.

Mads Mikkelsen currently has an estimated net worth of $14 million.

3 Donald Glover (Simba)

Donald Glover's Net Worth (Estimated) - $40 Million

A true multi-hyphenate, Donald Glover has conquered comedy, music, and film.

Known by his stage name, Childish Gambino, Glover first gained attention as a writer for the NBC sitcom 30 Rock, at the young age of 23.

He rose to fame with his portrayal of Troy Barnes in the NBC sitcom Community from 2009 to 2014.

Glover's creative versatility extends beyond acting to music, where he has achieved significant success as a rapper and singer.

His hit 2018 song "This Is America" had a profound impact, debuting atop the Billboard Hot 100 and earning him a Grammy.

Glover's role in the FX series Atlanta, which he created and directed, won him multiple Emmys and Golden Globes.

His film roles include playing Aaron Davis in Spider-Man: Homecoming and Lando Calrissian in Solo: A Star Wars Story.

Glover is also credited as a principal inspiration for the creation of the Marvel Comics superhero Miles Morales/Spider-Man, whom Glover himself voiced in the animated series Ultimate Spider-Man.

Between September 2018 and September 2019, Donald Glover earned more than $30 million from his diverse entertainment ventures, ranking him among the world's highest-paid entertainers during that period.

Notably, his 2018 This Is America Tour generated more than $16 million. In 2024, he created and starred in the Prime Video series Mr. & Mrs. Smith.

In Mufasa: The Lion King, Glover lends his voice to the adult Simba, reprising his role from the 2019 remake.

Donald Glover has an estimated net worth of $40 million.

2 Seth Rogen (Pumbaa)

Seth Rogen's Net Worth (Estimated) - $80 Million

Seth Rogen's spontaneity and distinctive comedic style have made him a Hollywood staple, with an estimated $80 million net worth.

Best known for his ability to blend humor with a touch of the everyday man's charm, Rogen has accumulated a wealth of experience both in front of and behind the camera.

Over his career, Rogen has starred in numerous films that have collectively grossed over $8.82 billion. His entry into the limelight began as a teenager in the realm of stand-up comedy, leading to his early acting break in Judd Apatow's Freaks And Geeks.

Rogen's significant breakout role came with the 2007 hit Knocked Up, directed by Apatow, which not only catapulted him into mainstream visibility but also showcased his potential as a leading man in Hollywood.

The film was a box office hit, grossing $219 million at the box office.

He continued this momentum by co-writing and starring in successful films like Superbad and Pineapple Express, further securing his standing as a versatile actor and skilled writer.

In Mufasa: The Lion King, Rogen reprises his role as Pumbaa, adding a vibrant and humorous layer to the film.
