Becky Worley Good Morning America, ABC News

Worley has remained a tech reporter/producer since 1998. She is currently the technology contributor for ABC’s Good Morning America. Worley is a contributor to Yahoo! Tech. She began her TV career immediately after college, working in news for Seattle’s KOMO-TV.

In 1998, the invention of an all technology network called ZDTV was a dream come true: TV + Tech. Within 6 years of her tenure at ZDTV /TechTV, Worley produced 3 different shows, hosted a live, hour-long computer help call-in show. By the time TechTV invented an 8-hour technology news format, Worley was appointed as an anchor.

She later started making guest appearances on Good Morning America as a tech expert. Worley was finally hired as the network’s technology contributor. She has been featured on World News with Diane Sawyer, Nightline, and 20/20 together with her regular reports for Good Morning America. Worley is a regular guest on the Dr. Oz Show.
