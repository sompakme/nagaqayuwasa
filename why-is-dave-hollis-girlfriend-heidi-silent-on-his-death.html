Why Is Dave Hollis Girlfriend Heidi Silent On His Death?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Expert fitness and transformation trainer Heidi Powell is the late Dave Hollis’ girlfriend. Heidi runs 60 days get fit challenge campaign and boot camp through her website.

Dave Hollis and Heidi Powell started dating in September 2020 after they met in Dave’s podcast and found that they both have many things in common.

Dave Hollis said goodbye much too soon to the world

Former Disney Distribution Chief Dave Hollis passed away at the age of 47. Dave was known for being romantically attached to the author and co-host of the ABC series Extreme Weight Loss, Heidi Powell.

Dave’s family member disclosed that he was at his residence at the time of his death in Austin on February 11, 2023.

Famous author Regan Long paid tribute to the former Disney Distribution Chief and wrote on her Facebook that her heart sank after discovering Dave Hollis had passed away.

Furthermore, as per Regan, his parents stated that Dave passed away peacefully at his house at the age of 47 and was suffering from heart issues. She asked the people to pray for Dave and his four children.

Sara Brown, a motivational speaker, also expressed her grief and paid tribute on her Facebook account by sharing Hollis’s picture and captioning, “I’m absolutely heartbroken for the friends and family of Dave Hollis. I keep thinking about his four precious children. On the eve of his 48th birthday, he said goodbye much too soon. Sending condolences and love to all grieving. Prayer warrior, please join me in praying over his children & family.”

However, his official cause of death has not been disclosed yet by his family or any social media source. 

A news magazine published that Former Disney Distribution Chief Dave Hollis was suffering from heart issues, for which he had to be hospitalized.

Everything you need to know about Dave Hollis’ girlfriend

Heidi Powell was born on March 28, 1982, in Flagstaff, Arizona, United States. At a very young age, she developed an interest in fitness and was hired as a part-time trainer while she was studying in college.

She started her career by working at the front desk at Gold’s Gym in Provo, Utah.

Powell also chased a career in real estate for almost six years, and after meeting her former husband, Chris Powell, she opted to build a brand name for herself in the fitness world.

She set her goal to become a personal trainer and acquired her ACE and CrossFit certifications. ABC invited Heidi and Chris to host an Extreme Weight Loss program, which made them familiar personalities among the public.

She and her former partner Chris also wrote a book, published with the name of “Extreme Transformation: Lifelong Weight Loss in 21 Days.” Heidi is also the owner of a clothing brand called Perfectly Imperfect.

Heidi Powell is the mother of four children. Before Chris, she was the wife of Derek Solomon, and however, she shares two children from each marriage. 

As per a social media post by Disney Distribution Chief Dave Hollis, he and Heidi started dating in September 2020. Hollis went through a lot after separating from his first wife, Rachel, and Heidi’s podcast and books helped him at that time.

Later, he invited her to his own podcast, and they decided to talk about divorce and life as a single parent. In another social media post, Hollis said that in their first meeting, they had a long conversation for around four hours and found that they had a lot in common.
