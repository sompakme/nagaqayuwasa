Wade Wilson – better known as Deadpool – not only entertains with his action, but also with his foul-mouthed, sharp, dagger-like tongue, from which no character is spared.

Audiences seem to enjoy his wit more than his action, and now Deadpool is making his Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) debut.

Deadpool (Ryan Reynolds) is fixated upon X-Men and the franchise's main character Wolverine (played by Hugh Jackman), and in the first Deadpool film, Wade makes a very in-character joke about it:

"You're probably thinking, 'Whose b***s did I have to fondle to get my very own movie?' I can’t tell you his name, but it rhymes with 'Polverine.' And let me tell you, he’s got a nice pair of smooth criminals down under."

Deadpool, at the start of the franchise's first movie, alluded to how he landed his very own superhero movie franchise — but in the Deadpool sequel, Wilson was clearly displeased with Wolverine, after the character died and took center stage.

At the very beginning of Deadpool 2, Wade says:

"F*** Wolverine. First, he rides my coattails with the R rating. Then the hairy motherf***** ups the ante by dying. What a d***. Well, guess what, Wolvie? I’m dying in this one, too!"

But now, Deadpool has seemingly somehow summoned Wolverine back from the clutches of death.

And Jackman, who played Wolverine/Logan, will appear in the third Deadpool film, Deadpool & Wolverine:

Fans of Wolverine and Deadpool are excited to see their contrasting relationship — Deadpool's playful bullying, and Wolverine's hot temper (and frequent inability to suppress it).

Deadpool & Wolverine's merger means both franchises' main characters will appear on-screen together.

Today, let’s see who will appear in Deadpool & Wolverine, how much they have amassed in terms of net worth, and what their roles will be in the upcoming, hotly-anticipated movie.

8 Emma Corrin

Emma Corrin's Net Worth (Estimated): $2 Million

The super-evil Cassandra Nova of Marvel Comics fame will appear in Deadpool & Wolverine as a notorious and extremely powerful villain.

The role of Cassandra Nova will be played by none other than highly talented actress Emma Corrin, who appeared as Princess Diana in The Crown, and won a Golden Globe for her brilliant performance.

In the comics, Cassandra Nova was the stillborn sister of Charles Xavier (the leader of the franchise's mutants), but she regenerated herself.

Fans were excited to discover that Cassandra Nova would serve as the main antagonist, as she is likely the most powerful mutant in the comics' universe.

Corrin's Nova has abilities including telepathy, DNA duplication, cell regeneration, telekinesis, mind and body exchange, soul gathering, cosmic fire, inferno control, and others.

It’s interesting to speculate as to how two superheroes (Wolverine and Deadpool) might cope with this mighty villain.

As of early 2024, Emma Corrin’s net worth is estimated at around $2 million, and it will surely increase in 2024.

Corrin is currently working on the horror/vampire film Nosferatu; she will play a scammer in Peaches, and, of course, Cassandra Nova in Deadpool & Wolverine.

Shioli Kutsuna's Net Worth (Estimated): $2 Million

A Japanese martial artist, the smiling and affable Yukio from Deadpool 2, will appear in the third installment of Deadpool.

Because of her incredible talent, she started to get more frequent roles in Hollywood, such as in Murder Mystery and the Invasion series.

Apart from Deadpool & Wolverine, this year she will feature as the main lead in Eugene The Marine.

The star now has a massive 54 acting projects to her credit, and she is just 32 years old. As of early 2024, Shioli Kutsuna's net worth is estimated at around $2 million.
