Rehab has become a common thing for celebrities to attend. For some celebrities, it is almost as if rehab is their version of a summer camp! However, all jokes aside, addiction is a real thing and celebrities seem to have a higher chance of suffering from addictions because they are exposed to so many more opportunities, and seemingly have endless amounts of money.

The world of show business is not like the real world. There is such easy access to drugs, alcohol and sex around every corner. That is just too much for a lot of stars to handle. The following ten celebrities have all suffered from sexual addiction. You may laugh, but it is in fact a very real thing, one that many people don't take seriously. Being addicted to sex is like being addicted to anything else, the need for it becomes more than anyone can handle, and the addiction takes over your body.

The ten celebrities which are featured in this article have all either been treated for, or have admitted to being addicted to sex. Although most of these addiction revelations happened after infidelity incidents, we will give them the benefit of the doubt and say that at least they are getting treatment for their affliction.

So sit back and enjoy getting to know which ten of your favorite celebrities are sex addicts.

10. Jesse James

Jesse James garnered some major heat when news came out that he had broken Sandra Bullock's heart. Who didn't hate him at least just a little bit? After all, Bullock is one of society's favourite actors.

Jesse James looks like a badass biker dude with his goatee, tattoos and black skulls, so he and Sandra Bullock were an odd couple from the start. The two officially separated in 2010 after rumors of his infidelity became public. In some sort of pathetic attempt to get her back, he went to rehab for what he called sex addiction. Although we are fully aware this is a real thing, we aren't 100% sure Jesse James isn't just a cheater.

9. Tiger Woods

Tiger Woods is by far one of the best and most infamous golfers to ever play. The thing about being in the public eye is that every move you make is not only watched, but is scrutinized by millions of people. Tiger Woods was one of those athletes that people truly looked up to, however after his countless infidelities surfaced people began to see this golf legend a little differently. Apparently Woods was as successful at rehab as he is on the golf course, and is now in recovery of his addiction. That being said, the damage is done and Tiger's image is always going to be altered by his cheating scandal, and apparent addiction to intercourse.

8. Charlie Sheen

Charlie Sheen is known for a lot of things, so of course he has a lot of addictions! In fact, addiction takes a new meaning when discussing Sheen! Sheen has battled an addiction to many of the more common drugs, as well as alcohol and of course sex. The actor is probably the most out of control person in the world and he knows it. Sheen has been to rehab several times, but he won't stop partying unless he chooses too! Although he seems to be a little off his rocker, something tells me that he is the kind of person that will just always be addicted to life! One thing is for sure, he is certainly one of the most interesting celebrities to write about.

7. Eric Benet

Halle Berry is arguably one of the most beautiful actresses in Hollywood. Imagine being married to her and cheating? And yet that's what happened! The world was surprised when her then-husband Eric Benet was discovered as a cheater. The R&B singer sought help at a sex addiction treatment center after Berry found out about the infidelities. In 2004, despite his attempts at treatment, the couple split. He has since denied that he is an addict, but as most people know, denial is a big part of addiction. It is very common for sex addicts to minimize their sexual behaviour, and in the process ignore the damage it has caused to their life.

6. Nicole Narain

One of the only females to make this list is the famous Playboy model Nicole Narain. Nicole sought help for her sex addiction in 2009 on VH1's reality show Sex Rehab with Dr. Drew. Throughout her journey, she revealed some pretty intimate details about her addiction. Narain admitted to chronic self-stimulation, which is extremely common with addicts as well as involvement in numerous sexual experiences and escapades. In 2005, a dirty tape with actor Colin Farrell surfaced the web, shocking numerous people. Nicole Narain is probably not the only women in Hollywood to suffer from this addiction, but she is one of the only ones to have the courage to speak about it.

5. David Duchovny

This handsome actor is best known for his role on his TV show Californication where he plays a sex-obsessed writer. The role became a lot more real for the actor in 2009 when David entered rehab for being addicted to the deed as well. As you can imagine, he and his wife, actress Tea Leoni, faced a lot of hardships after news of his addiction came out. Although they took a step back from the relationship, they have since reconciled. Duchovny is lucky because it is very important for couples to work together in order to get through any addiction. You just need to hope their love is strong enough to survive that tough experience.

4. Kari Ann Peniche

Kari is no stranger to having adult situations come to the public eye! In 2004, Kari Ann Peniche lost her crown as Miss United States Teen after she posed nude for Playboy.

If that wasn't enough, in 2009, she was involved in a naughty tape scandal with actors Rebecca Gayheart and Eric Dane. Since these events transpired, Peniche has admitted to having a few problems with both substance and intercourse addiction, but has sought out treatment. While you may not hear as many women who battle this kind of addiction, it is absolutely a disease that impacts both genders.

3. Amy Winehouse

Amy Winehouse was without a doubt one of the most talented singers of this generation. Her tragic death in 2011 shook both her fans and the music industry. Winehouse suffered an overdose, but she also had a very addictive personality and had an optimistic career ahead of her. Winehouse very publicly battled her addiction to alcohol and drugs, but apparently she had another vice she couldn't live without. Amy Winehouse is claimed to have had a huge sexual appetite and that she had numerous fetishes. Although she died at the young age of 27, Winehouse was still able to live an incredibly interesting life.

1. Michael Douglas

Michael Douglas is known to be one of the first celebrities to publicly announce that he had a sex addiction. In 1990, he was admitted to an addiction treatment center which brought the idea and concept of sexual addiction to the public eye. Douglas, who married the stunning actress Catherine Zeta-Jones in 2000, claims that reports of his situation were inaccurate. Whether he means that it was completely fabricated or just exaggerated we are not sure, but you can imagine that it's frustrating to have things about of your life be made up or overblown.

Like most addicts, there is a sense of embarrassment that comes with the disease, but not all addicts have to deal with the paparazzi!
