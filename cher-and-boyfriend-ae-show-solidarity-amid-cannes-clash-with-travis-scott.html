Cher And Boyfriend AE Show Solidarity Amid Cannes Clash With Travis Scott

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Cher and AE have a strong bond despite their 40-year age gap, proven through their public affection and resilience.
  • An intense brawl at Cannes involving Travis Scott, Tyga, and AE occurred at a party hosted by Richie Akiva.
  • After the fight, Cher and AE were seen sharing a kiss on the red carpet, showing their commitment and relationship strength.

Cher, the iconic actress and singer, has been in a relationship with rapper Alexander AE Edwards for almost two years. Their unique bond has captivated fans and the media, with constant speculation surrounding their romance.

Despite their differences, most notably their 40-year age difference, the superstar pair have shared a deep connection. The couple, who never seem to shy away from engaging in public displays of affection, recently had their second major scandal in their relationship when AE was involved in a brawl with two other famous rappers in Cannes.

Rapper AE Was In An Intense Fight At Cannes Involving Travis Scott And Tyga

Cher and boyfriend Alexander "AE" Edwards made headlines after news broke out that the rapper was involved in an intense altercation last Friday.

The shocking incident took place at an after-party held by nightlife guru Richie Akiva in Cannes to celebrate the Cannes Film Festival. According to an exclusive report in PageSix, the fight occurred after the party's host, Richie Akiva, gave a shout-out to rappers Travis Scott, Tyga, and the latter's close friend AE as his special guests, which allegedly irritated Scott.

As Tyga and AE were by the DJ booth, guests state that Scott approached them with a friend, who then pushed AE. The fight got worse when AE engaged in a scuffle with Travis Scott, who snatched a microphone out of Akiva's hand moments earlier. It escalated, and Tyga was pulled into the battle.

According to TMZ, although the fight was intense, no one was seriously harmed as security had managed to break things up in time.

As fans of the rappers will know, Travis Scott and Tyga once dated Kylie Jenner, and the former shares two kids with her, daughter Stormie and son Aire. According to several notable publications, the talented rapper's close association with Jenner may have fueled the incident.

Cher And Boyfriend AE Pack On The PDA Following Cannes Brawl Incident

Despite the intense brawl at Cannes, AE's relationship with Cher remains strong, a testament to their resilience and commitment to each other.

The two love birds shocked photographers when they shared a passionate kiss on the red carpet for the amfAR gala a day before.
