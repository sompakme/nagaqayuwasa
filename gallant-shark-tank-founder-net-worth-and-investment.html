What is Gallant and Who is the founder of Gallant?

Gallant is a company that specializes in preserving stem cells for pets. They work with pet owners and veterinarians to collect and store pet stem cells for future use in case their pets develop certain health conditions. The founder and CEO of Gallant is Aaron Hirschhorn, who was inspired to start the company after successfully undergoing stem cell treatment for his back pain.

Stem cell therapy for pets has shown promising results in treating various ailments, including arthritis, joint injuries, and certain chronic conditions. How Does Gallant Work?

Gallant offers a reliable and efficient stem cell banking service for pets, ensuring the preservation and accessibility of pet stem cells for potential regenerative therapy. The process begins during routine spaying or neutering procedures, where stem cells are collected from the pets. These valuable cells are then meticulously processed and frozen in Gallant’s state-of-the-art FDA-approved laboratory, guaranteeing their long-term viability and effectiveness.

When the time comes for potential therapeutic intervention, the stored pet stem cells are readily available for use. Whether it’s addressing degenerative diseases, injuries, or other health conditions, the stored stem cells can be deployed to promote regeneration and facilitate the healing process in pets.

How it Works

The process is simple. When pet owners choose Gallant’s stem cell banking service, veterinarians collect a small sample of stem cells from the pet during a routine spaying or neutering procedure. Should the pet require stem cell therapy in the future, these stored stem cells can be readily accessed for treatment.

Gallant’s stem cell banking service offers peace of mind to pet owners, knowing that they have a proactive measure in place to provide the best possible care for their furry companions.
